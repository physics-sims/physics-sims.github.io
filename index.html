SHIFT Simulations

Anonymized review materials for a manuscript submitted to The Physics Teacher.

Phase 1 — Manual kinematics

  • Constant Velocity (uniform motion)
  • Free Fall Flower (free fall)

Phase 2 — Havok engine and manual approximations

  • Relative Velocity (relative motion)
  • Friction Skill (static friction)
  • Pendulum Goal (pendulum motion)
  • Faraday Game (electromagnetic induction)
  • Carnot Game (thermodynamic cycles)

Phase 3 — AI-assisted modeling

  • Snell Laser (geometric optics)

Notes for the reviewer

  • Each simulation runs entirely in the browser, with no installation required.
  • The simulations use a fixed 9:16 portrait layout. For the most authentic experience, view them on a smartphone or resize your browser window to a vertical (portrait) aspect ratio.
  • Touch (mobile) and mouse-click (desktop) inputs are both supported.

All identifying information (author name, institution, portal name) has been anonymized for double-blind review. Original references and credits will be restored upon acceptance.
